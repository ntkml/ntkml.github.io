The Get In Touch Foundation: The Daisy Wheel


the ask.

- Achieve 500k unique downloads for The Daisy Wheel - within the first year
- Cultivate a new generation of young adults into the Get In Touch brand

the problem.

Womxn are inundated with so many "pink things" for breast cancer awareness that pink has almost lost its meaning all together. How do we make it that when womxn see pink, they don't think awareness but are, instead, called to action to perform self breast examinations with the Daisy Wheel app?
