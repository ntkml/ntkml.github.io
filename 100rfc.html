the problem.

Many people of color face a different standard when it comes to their physical appearance in the workplace. Women and men, experience this bias in different ways and at different levels. In the patriarchal society we live in, womxn, especially BlPOC are further scrutinized based on their appearance.

the ask.

- Open an honest conversation around appearance and racial bias in the workplace and the outside world regarding BIPOC.
- Develop an organic social campaign that tags professionals of color to show what we look like out our element.
